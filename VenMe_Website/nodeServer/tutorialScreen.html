Step 1: This is the first thing you will see when you click on Create Qr.

Step 2: Now place your cursor in the text box and enter you info just like how the example shows it.

Step 3: Once you have entered your info just like the example you can hit enter on the keyboard, this will generate your QR code.

Step 4: Now you have two options, you can right click and save to your computer, or you can open the QR code in another tab to print it.

Step 5: If you picked to open in a new tab this is what your QR code will look like.

Step 6: Now you can right click and pick the print option.
